PopStar Game Introduction：Pop Star is a casual puzzle game, It released on google play and Apple app store. The latest version includes classic mode and original endless mode gameplay.

Furious Tank Game Introduction：Furious Tank is a realistic pixel-art tank shooter that redefines the classic 2D top-down experience. It released on google play and Apple app store. Featuring high-definition 64*64 pixel units, the game delivers intense, visceral armored combat. Experience a living battlefield with a dynamic day-night cycle, diverse terrain, and shifting weather conditions. From gritty frontline skirmishes to the devastating deployment of tactical missiles and nuclear strikes, Fury Tanks is the modern rebirth of retro tank warfare.
